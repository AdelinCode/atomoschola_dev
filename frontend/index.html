Become a Creator

Want to create quality educational content? Here's how you can become a creator:

  1. Propose an original lesson on a subject you're passionate about
  2. Our commission will evaluate the quality and relevance of your proposed content
  3. If your lesson is approved, you'll receive Creator status
  4. Start publishing lessons and contributing to the educational community

Each approved lesson strengthens your reputation as a valuable content creator!

Become an Editor

Have a keen eye for detail and want to improve existing content? Here's how to become an editor:

  1. Propose 3 edits to 3 different lessons on the platform
  2. Your edits should improve clarity, accuracy, or overall content quality
  3. The commission will evaluate your contributions and attention to detail
  4. If all 3 edits are accepted, you'll join the editorial team

Editors play an essential role in maintaining high quality standards!
